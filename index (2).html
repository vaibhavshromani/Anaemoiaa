NAKSHATRA

✦ Inspired by the 27 nakshatras of Indian astrology, Nakshatra is the debut couture collection by Anemoia — a celestial exploration of femininity, destiny, and emotion. The collection unfolds through 27 looks, each representing the distinct essence of a nakshatra: its energy, symbolism, mood, and spiritual depth. Moving between softness and strength, stillness and transformation, every silhouette reflects a different facet of the feminine spirit. Fluid drapes, celestial embellishments, intricate craftsmanship, and sculptural forms come together to create garments that feel ethereal yet deeply rooted in heritage.
The final look emerges as the soul of the collection: a bridal ensemble symbolizing culmination, rebirth, and eternal connection. Like the last star completing a constellation, it marks the closing chapter of Nakshatra while opening the beginning of a new journey. Each garment exists as an ode to cosmic alignment — where tradition meets avant-garde, and every stitch honors the lunar mansions that guide us.

⸻ 27 celestial silhouettes, one eternal story.
